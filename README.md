## 🔍 What is ModelForge?  

ModelForge is a desktop application that runs on your Windows computer. It lets you use AI tools locally without needing the internet. Built around Ollama, the app offers features like chat, downloads, memory, and uses your computer effectively. It helps you manage AI models easily and interact with them through a simple interface.  

You don’t need any programming knowledge to use it. ModelForge works quietly in the background and lets you focus on your tasks.  

## 🖥️ System Requirements  

To run ModelForge smoothly on your Windows PC, check the following:  

- Windows 10 or newer (64-bit)  
- At least 8 GB of RAM  
- Minimum 10 GB free disk space  
- Intel i5 or AMD Ryzen 5 processor, or better  
- Internet connection for downloading models (not required to run once installed)  

If your PC meets these specs, ModelForge will run without issues.
